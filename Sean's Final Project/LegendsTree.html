    Darth Bane (formerly Dessel)
    Master: None (creator of the Rule of Two)

    Apprentice: Darth Zannah

    Description: Darth Bane is the originator of the Rule of Two. He was a former miner who sought power and became one of the most influential Sith Lords. His primary aim was to reshape the Sith Order, focusing on secrecy and strength. He believed that constant infighting weakened the Sith and created the Rule of Two to ensure stability and a clearer path to power.

    Darth Zannah
    Master: Darth Bane
    Apprentice: Darth Cognus

    Description: Darth Zannah, originally known as Rain, was Bane's apprentice. She was a skilled Sith who used dark side powers, including Sith alchemy and illusions. After Bane's death, she continued his legacy and maintained the Rule of Two, ensuring the survival of the Sith Order.

    Darth Cognus
    Master: Darth Zannah
    Apprentice: Darth Millennial

    Description: Darth Cognus was Zannah's apprentice and became the Sith Master after her. Known for her ruthlessness and cunning, Cognus focused on continuing the Sith's legacy of secrecy and manipulation. She played a significant role in preserving the Sith Order through the turbulent times following her rise to power.

    Darth Millennial
    Master: Darth Cognus
    Description: Darth Millennial was unique among the Sith as he chose to reject the traditional Sith goals and doctrines. He believed in reforming the Sith Order and eventually went into self-imposed exile. His tenure was marked by a controversial departure from the established norms of the Rule of Two.

    Darth Vectivus
    Apprentice: None (died before taking an apprentice)
    Description: Darth Vectivus was a Sith Lord known for his business acumen and desire for wealth. He operated in secrecy and maintained a low profile compared to other Sith Lords. His death occurred before he could take on an apprentice, making him a somewhat obscure figure within the Rule of Two lineage.

    Darth Plagueis(Darth Plagueis the Wise)
    Master: Unknown (assumed to be a previous Sith Lord before his own ascension)
    Apprentice: Darth Sidious (Emperor Palpatine)

    Darth Plagueis, also known as Darth Plagueis the Wise, was a Sith Lord known for his profound knowledge of the dark side of the Force and his obsession with achieving immortality. His teachings focused on manipulating the Force to prevent death and create life, which he believed would give the Sith ultimate power. Plagueis's reign was marked by his attempts to find ways to cheat death, but he was ultimately betrayed and killed by his own apprentice, Darth Sidious, who took over as the Sith Master. Plagueis's story is a significant part of the Sith lore, as his quest for immortality and the knowledge he imparted had a lasting impact on the Sith Order.

    Darth Sidious(Emperor Palpatine)
    Master: Darth Plagueis
    Apprentice: Darth Maul, later Darth Tyranus, and finally Darth Vader

    Description: Darth Sidious, also known as Emperor Palpatine, is one of the most prominent Sith Lords in history. Under his apprenticeship, he orchestrated the fall of the Jedi and the rise of the Galactic Empire. His ability to manipulate galactic politics and his role in the execution of Order 66 were pivotal in his success.

    Darth Maul
    Master: Darth Sidious
    Apprentice: None

    Description: Darth Maul was known for his distinctive appearance, including his red and black tattoos and double-bladed lightsaber. Initially, he served as an apprentice to Darth Sidious but was later betrayed and left for dead. Despite this, he survived and continued to seek vengeance against the Jedi and the Sith.

    Darth Tyranus(Count Dooku)
    Master: Darth Sidious
    Apprentice: None

    Description: Darth Tyranus, formerly known as Count Dooku, was a former Jedi Master who became Sidious's apprentice. He played a key role in the Clone Wars and was instrumental in the formation of the Confederacy of Independent Systems. His tenure as a Sith Lord was marked by his strategic mind and powerful combat skills.

    Darth Vader(Anakin Skywalker)
    Master: Darth Sidious
    Apprentice: None (later redeemed)

    Description: Darth Vader, originally Anakin Skywalker, was a former Jedi Knight who fell to the dark side and became Sidious's apprentice. He played a crucial role in the Jedi Purge and the rise of the Galactic Empire. His story is one of redemption, as he ultimately turned against Sidious and was redeemed by his son, Luke Skywalker.
