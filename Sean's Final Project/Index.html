    • Welcome to My Tribute to Darth Bane

    Bane Legends is a tribute to one of the most fascinating sith in the Star Wars universe. Darth Bane's journey, from his rise to power, to his pivotal role in shaping the Sith, has always captivated me. The Bane books were a huge part of my introduction to the expanded Star Wars universe. His story is not just a tale of dark power but a complex narrative of ambition, strategy, and transformation.

    The Darth Bane trilogy by Drew Karpyshyn were a huge part of my introduction to the expanded Star Wars universe and left a lasting impression on me. Bane stands out as a major figure in the Sith legacy. This site is my way of celebrating the impact these books had on me and sharing this legendary character with new and fellow fans.

    Thank you for visiting,
    and may the Dark Side be with you!
